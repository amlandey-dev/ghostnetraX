OFFLINE AI SETUP
How to download the local WiFi model
for offline AI chat.
01

OPEN THE APP

Launch GhostNetraX Pro on your Android device. Make sure you have an active internet connection for the initial model download.

02

OPEN THE CHAT PANEL

Tap the 🗨️ icon to open the AI chat interface.

03

DOWNLOAD THE OFFLINE AI MODEL

In the message input area — the box where you type and send messages to the AI bot — find and tap the Download Offline AI option to begin downloading the model.

04

WAIT FOR THE DOWNLOAD

Wait a few minutes or seconds (depending on your internet speed) for the model to finish downloading. Once complete, tap the 📊 icon to open the Overview panel.

05

VERIFY & START USING

In the Overview, check the ENGINE STATUS section. When your offline AI model has been downloaded successfully, the OFFLINE MODEL status will show READY. You're now able to experience fully offline AI chat — no internet required!
